Greetings Codeforces!

sammyuri, myst-6 and I are absolutely chuffed to bits to invite you all to have a go at Codeforces Round 1210 (Spectral::Cup 2026 Round 2 (Codeforces Round 1100, Div. 1 + Div. 2)), kicking off at Saturday, May 23, 2026 at 20:05UTC+5.5.

There will be 9 absolute cinema problems for you to solve over the course of 3 hours, some problems will have multiple parts. This round will be combined for Division 1 and Division 2 and will be rated for everyone.
